- Your grade depends first on whether you choose an interesting use case and match it with a suitable solution. The project should solve a clear real-world problem, and the solution should use a customised open-source LLM that your group controls. Your grade also depends on the overall completeness and quality of execution. You should build a full end-to-end system, try at least two genuinely different techniques, and use evaluation results to explain why you chose your final approach. The final grading criterion is how well you go beyond the basics in one area. After getting the full pipeline working, your group should focus on improving one aspect, such as data quality, training methods, or inference efficiency.
